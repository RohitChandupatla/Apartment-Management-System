About Project

This system is divided into three sections: administrative, apartment owner, and user. The administrator will be in charge of all apartment activities, while the owner will be able to keep track of his flat's information and rent payments. The people that dwell in the apartment are known as users. If they are tenants of the units, they can file complaints, verify activity, and examine their rent statements
